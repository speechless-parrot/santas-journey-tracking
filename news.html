🎅✨ Santa’s Journey Tracking Officially Launches! 🌍🎄

September 6, 2025

The elves have been hard at work, Santa’s sleigh is polished, the reindeer are fueled up on magical oats, and today we are proud to announce the official launch of Santa’s Journey Tracking! 🛷🌟

This brand-new tracker brings families, friends, and Christmas fans together from all over the world to follow Santa’s magical journey on Christmas Eve. Starting at 8:30 AM UTC on December 24th, you’ll be able to see Santa’s final pre-flight preparations live from the North Pole. Then, at 10:00 AM UTC, the sleigh takes off, and the adventure begins! 🎄🎁

But that’s not all—our tracker goes beyond just Santa’s location. You’ll find:

  • ✨ Real-time updates from Santa and the elves
  • ✨ A cozy festive map with Santa waving from his sleigh
  • ✨ Stats on presents delivered, distance traveled, and Santa’s ETA to your home
  • ✨ A whole collection of games, activities, and North Pole fun to enjoy while waiting

This isn’t just about tracking—it’s about making Christmas magic come alive in a whole new way. Families can follow along together, explore the North Pole Village, and keep the spirit of the season alive long after the presents are unwrapped. 🎁❤️

So get ready, mark your calendars, and grab your hot cocoa—it’s time to track Santa like never before. Welcome to the future of Christmas magic. 🎅✨
